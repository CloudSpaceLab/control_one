Security & trust

Built like infrastructure security teams expect

Zero standing privilege

Every privileged session is JIT and time-bound. SSH certs are signed by a tenant CA and expire in minutes.

Encrypted at rest

AES-256-GCM on every credential, API key, and SSH CA private key. Operator never sees plaintext after save.

Step-up MFA

TOTP and WebAuthn (FIDO2) for the highest-risk actions: rotate CA, approve change-window override, delete tenant.

Tamper-evident audit

Append-only audit log with actor, target, action, and metadata. Mapped to SOC 2 CC and ISO 27001 controls.

WireGuard mesh

Optional encrypted overlay between control plane and nodes. Bastion never traverses the public internet.

Open formats

tlog session recordings, OpenTelemetry traces, OIDC auth, standard SSH certs. No proprietary lock-in.

Air-gap ready

Self-contained install bundle. Offline threat-feed mirroring. No phone-home telemetry without consent.

RBAC & multi-tenant

Role-based access at the page, route, and action level. Tenants are isolated from the database up.

FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot

How does this compare to CrowdStrike, Palo Alto, or Okta?

Control One deploys intelligent robots across your entire fleet. Unlike point solutions, our robots give you complete visibility, autonomous control, and tamper-evident proof in one dashboard with unique IP intelligence capabilities.

What's the implementation timeline?

Most teams deploy robots in under 30 minutes. Bootstrap the control plane, enrol your first hosts, and start seeing robot data immediately. Full fleet enrolment depends on your infrastructure size.

Do I need to replace my existing SIEM?

No. Control One robots can work alongside your existing SIEM. The Apache Doris backbone handles millions of events per day, and you can forward events to Splunk, Loki, or Elastic if needed.

How do you handle air-gapped environments?

One tarball, no outbound calls. The offline bundle ships every robot binary, migration, and threat-feed mirror you need. The included docker-compose runs Postgres, Redis, the control plane, and the operator console with no internet egress.

What's your security posture?

Zero standing privilege, AES-256-GCM encryption at rest, step-up MFA for high-risk actions, tamper-evident audit logs, and WireGuard mesh for encrypted robot communication. We practice what we preach.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes. The Starter tier is free for up to 50 hosts. Book a demo and we'll spin up a sandbox with your data to show you exactly how our robots solve your biggest headaches.

What happens if I exceed my host count?

On the Business tier, you have unlimited hosts. On the Starter tier, you can upgrade at any time. We'll notify you before you hit limits.

Do you offer government/compliance-specific versions?

Yes. The Enterprise tier includes FedRAMP/IRAP scoping, air-gapped deployment, and custom integrations for regulated environments.
